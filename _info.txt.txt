F1 App Project
--------------

- Home
	- Last Race info
		https://ergast.com/api/f1/current/last/results.json (last results)
	- Upcoming Race info
	
- Drivers
	- List all drivers
		https://ergast.com/api/f1/2022/drivers.json
	- OnClick, list driver detail
		https://ergast.com/api/f1/drivers/alonso.json
	
- Races
	- List all races (race schedule)
		https://ergast.com/api/f1/2022.json
		https://ergast.com/api/f1/current.json
		
	- OnClick, list race/circuit details
		https://ergast.com/api/f1/2022/5/results.json (by the round number)		
	
- Constructors
	- List all constructor
		https://ergast.com/api/f1/2022/constructors.json
	- OnClick, show team info and the drivers. Head to Head
		http://ergast.com/api/f1/constructors/mclaren.json
	
- Standings
	- Driver Standings
		https://ergast.com/api/f1/2022/driverStandings.json
	- Constructor Standings
		https://ergast.com/api/f1/2022/constructorStandings.json
